Why the heat

Practice that transforms

Heat is not just a setting — it is a teacher. It asks more of you, and gives more back.

01
Deep detoxification

Practising in 38–40°C opens the pores and encourages a deep, cleansing sweat that flushes toxins and leaves skin glowing.

02
Injury prevention

Warm muscles stretch further and recover faster, dramatically reducing the risk of injury and building long-term joint resilience.

03
Mental clarity

The intensity of heat demands full presence. Students leave with a profound sense of calm, focus and mental reset.

04
Cardiovascular strength

Heat elevates heart rate and metabolism, delivering cardiovascular benefits comparable to moderate aerobic exercise.

Our philosophy
"The body achieves what the mind believes."

Bodyforme was founded in 2014 by two practitioners who believed that the best studio in the city should also be its most welcoming. We built a space where beginners and seasoned yogis practice side by side — without judgement, without ego, with total commitment.

Every element of the studio — from the timber floors to the filtered humidity — has been carefully considered to maximise your practice and your comfort.
