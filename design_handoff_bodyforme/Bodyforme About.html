Our story

Born from a practice

Sarah and James Hollis discovered Bikram yoga in 2009, each independently, and each for very different reasons. Sarah was recovering from a back injury. James was looking for something — he still isn't entirely sure what. Within six months, they had both found it, and found each other.

In 2014, after years of training and a relentless search for the right space, they opened Bodyforme on Smith Street. The studio was small. The community was immediate. Within two years they had expanded, added Hot Pilates, and built a team of instructors they describe as "the best humans we know".

Today Bodyforme is one of Melbourne's most-loved wellness studios — not because of the programmes, but because of the people. Students who started as beginners now teach here. Friendships forged in the heat room have become marriages, businesses, and lifelong bonds.

What we believe

The values we practise

These aren't words on a wall. They shape how we teach, how we welcome, and how we build this community every single day.

01
Heat as teacher

The room doesn't lie. Heat removes distraction and forces presence. We believe there is no better environment for genuine self-discovery.

02
No ego

Everyone was a beginner once. We celebrate the person attempting their first half-moon as loudly as the person attempting their hundredth.

03
Community first

We are not a transactional gym. We are a community. We remember names, celebrate milestones and show up for each other outside the studio too.

04
Honest teaching

Our instructors correct and challenge. They don't offer empty encouragement — they offer the kind of feedback that actually creates change.

05
The long game

We are not interested in quick transformations. We build practices that last decades — because the benefits of consistent, mindful movement compound over a lifetime.

06
Space to fail

Falling out of a posture is not failure. It's information. We create an environment where trying is always celebrated, regardless of outcome.

Our journey

Twelve years of heat

From a single room to a full studio, from two founders to eight instructors, from a handful of students to a community of thousands.

2014
Bodyforme opens its doors Sarah and James launch the studio on Smith Street with two instructors, one heat room, and a vision for what community wellness could look like.
2016
Hot Pilates launches Angie Torres joins the team and introduces the Hot Pilates programme — within three months it is the most booked class in the studio.
2018
Studio expansion Growing demand leads to a full expansion — a second heat room, dedicated Pilates studio and an entirely redesigned reception and lounge.
2020
Through the difficult years The pandemic closed the doors for months. The community responded with unwavering loyalty — and reopening day was the most emotional morning in the studio's history.
2022
Sculpt Yoga & Special Forces Two new programmes launch, responding to member feedback for more variety. Both sell out within the first week.
2026
Today — 4,200+ members Bodyforme turns twelve. The community is bigger, warmer and more committed than ever. We're just getting started.
