Questions

Common questions

Can't find what you're looking for? We're always happy to help.

Get in touch →
Is the first week really free? +

Yes — completely free, with no credit card required to claim it. New members only. You can attend unlimited classes during your trial week across any class type. The offer is available to first-time members only and runs until 31 May 2026.

Can I pause or cancel my membership? +

Absolutely. Foundation members can pause for up to 4 weeks per year. Immersion members can pause for up to 8 weeks. All memberships can be cancelled at any time with 7 days notice — no fees, no questions.

What happens if I miss a class? +

For Foundation and Immersion memberships, unused classes in a week do not roll over. For Casual packs, your credits never expire so there's no pressure. We ask that cancellations are made at least 2 hours before class time to avoid forfeiting the credit.

Do I need experience to join? +

No experience is needed. All of our classes are suitable for complete beginners unless specifically marked otherwise. We recommend arriving 10 minutes early for your first class so our team can show you around and answer any questions.

Can I change my membership plan? +

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your membership at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing week. Just contact us or make the change directly through the Bodyforme app.

What should I bring to my first class? +

Bring a large water bottle, a yoga mat (we have mats to hire for $3), a towel and comfortable activewear. For Bikram classes, lightweight, minimal clothing is recommended — it gets warm. Lockers are available in the change rooms.
